First run

Set up the reading tool, then forget the setup.

Steeped turns the current page into a short note with sources. It only reads when you ask.

  1. 1Add your Anthropic key. It stays in Chrome.
  2. 2Pick a palette for the side panel.
  3. 3Open the page you meant to read. Click Steeped or right-click the page.
  4. 4Optional: use the shortcut if you like keeping your hands on the keyboard.

When to use it
Best for pages with enough substance to summarize and enough value to come back to.
Long articles and essays Get the argument before you decide whether to read every paragraph.
Technical docs and changelogs Pull out behavior changes, caveats, and the part that affects your work.
Research pages, filings, and launch posts Turn dense facts into a short note with citations still attached.
Issue threads and discussions Find the decision, current status, and the comments worth opening.
